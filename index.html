About Us

We the Zambia National Association for Saw-millers And Timber Industries have collectively resolved to maintain the smooth running of members’ business across the country of Zambia and beyond in all harvesting, movement, processing and trade of plantation and indigenous timber and in so doing shall be guided by the Laws of the Country especially the Forest Act No. 4 of 2015 or any amendment made thereto in proving the legality of their activities and of Timber supply. The Zambia National Association for Saw-millers and Timber Industries has four main Organs, namely Annual General Meeting, National Executive Committee, Provincial Executive Committee and the District Executive Committee. The National Executive Committee chaired by the president, examines and approves questions and decisions relative to the Association’s strategy and general organization Read more...


Our Objectives

Promote the use and application of timber and/ or forest-based products and encourage the growing of trees, setting up of small holder, private and community forest plantations in the public interest

Promote the environmental benefits of timber as a natural material and the benefits of sustainable forestry, in the public interest;

Assist its members in the establishment of markets for their products, find land for establishment of timber processing and value adding clusters;

Recommend members for renewal of round wood contracts/ concession licenses subject to such members meeting the requirements of a round timber contract/ concession licenses;
